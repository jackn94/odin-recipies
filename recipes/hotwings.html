Hot wings

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken wings
  • nados peri-peri hot sauce
  • chilli powder
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Rub vegetable oil on chicken wings
  2. Rub chilli powder then nandos hot sauce on chicken wings
  3. Leave to soak in large bowl for at least 1 hour
  4. Heat oven to 180 C
  5. Put chicken wings in oven (keep any remaining sauce in bowl)
  6. After 30 mins add any left over sauce to wings and turn them once
  7. Cook for an additional 15 minutes while checking occasionally
  8. Enjoy
